The artist

Ash's
POV

I work across drawing, writing, and photography — three ways of looking at the same thing. Pencil and charcoal for the slow seeing, words for what the image leaves behind, and a camera for the moments I can't draw fast enough.

My practice lives in the quiet space between light and shadow. I'm drawn to faces, empty rooms, and the grammar of everyday things — the way a hand rests on a table, the way a corridor holds its breath.

Based wherever the light is interesting.
